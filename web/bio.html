NAOMI TURE is a director, producer & digital artist living and working in the Bay Area, CA. Her wide-ranging background in the digital arts includes video & television production, educational technology, animation, installation and graphic design.

Currently working as Senior Producer for KMVT Community Television in Mountain View, CA, Naomi directs and produces professional videos for the station, teaches digital arts classes, oversees the internship program, and assists in covering community events.

Beginning her career in Rhode Island, she co-developed a pre-operative software package for the Hasbro Children's Hospital. In New York, her experience included the production of a 5.1 surround sound video for the Schenectady Planetarium and 3D architectural animations for Morse Zehnter Associates. Her first stint in California resulted in an online exhibit for the Exploratorium called "Step Re-Mix". Naomi is interested in the intersection of education, human perception and creative technologies.

Naomi holds a degree in Education Studies from Brown University and a Masters in Electronic Art from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.
