Global Lives Project — 2010

The exhibit displays 24-hours in the lives of 10 people around the world who loosely represent the demographic diversity of our planet.

As Production Committee Chair, I helped to oversee the production of 240 hours of footage, from 10 countries, from pre-production to implementation in the exhibit. Our Yerba Buena Center for the Arts World Premiere opened in February 2010 with over 1,100 guests attending.
